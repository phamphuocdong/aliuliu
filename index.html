Project!!

This is my ideas about a project which might be helpful for students.

Overview.

My project ideas are to make an application about education named Efficient Learning (EL). It can be used by parents, students, and teachers in order to support students to gain the best learning results. This is the studying application that is easy to use and people will probably feel that this is the tool that can help them study better. At some of the first few versions of this application, it just contains all of the content that we take from the official textbook of Vietnamese students from grades 1 to 12. Later on, if any countries want to collaborate with me, I will add their educational content to my application so they can also use it for their students.

Motivation.

My motivation for making this application is because when I am still studying from grade 1 to grade 12, I do not have any online resources that can support my studies. I have to rely on the textbook most of the time but it is hard for me to understand the content in these textbooks without help from teachers. And when I started to study at RMIT, I have an opportunity to use online learning for my studies and that is the time and realized that these online sources are so helpful. Therefore, I want Vietnamese students to also have a chance to get an advantage from online learning and all of my ideas for this application are come from this thought.

Feature1: The Connection.

This application is a connection between every people because it allows parents, teachers, and students to create their own accounts. Specifically, each type of user has their own way to use it. For example, as a student, they can use the app to study, have a discussion with their friend, and asking teachers if there have any questions about their studies in school. As a teacher, through this application, they can also discuss with their students, support their students whenever they need and also contact their parents. Finally, as a parent, they can follow their child’s studies, their behavior at school through teacher’s feedback. This is very important, it seems like most of the parents spend most of the time working and forgot to notice how their child’s studies. Thus, this application will be the middle place where students, parents, and teachers can understand more about each other.

Feature2: Online Admission.

After students enroll in their course, they can access a lot of course materials, including online listening, reading, videos, exercises, and many other learning resources. Back in the past, Vietnamese students can only learn from their textbooks and that means they always keep the textbooks with them if they want to study. This application will allow students to study whenever they want and wherever they stay with only their tablet, phone, or any other mobile devices.

Feature3: Interesting learning resources.

Students will probably find it is much more interesting when they use this application to study. For instance, students often feel bored in a literature class or history class. In order to help students find it more interested in these subjects, the application provides lots of videos, images about the story or historical event and avoid showing so many words which will easily make students hate studying. In addition, there are many mini-games that students can play with their classmates to review the lesson that they learn in class like a puzzle game.

Feature4: Easily manage your study.

By using this application, students may find it easier to manage their studies. The application includes a calendar and it will strongly suggest students make their own study schedule. Once students finished making their study schedule, every day, the application will notice the user what to do at certain times of the day. This is probably the key for students to get better learning results. Besides that, the app displays a list of achievements that students can see it as a goal to study. Students can get these achievements through the study activities and exercise in the application. Moreover, through students’ assignment scores and the result from the exercise of the app, it will base on the total result and show students what feature in study that they good at, are they on track or not, and what are their weak points that they need to be put in more effort.

Those software that I looking for to do my project helps the user create their apps easier by using special tools that they provide. I think this project is quite feasible because I already have some software from the internet that can help me create my app. Moreover, I will soon study about some coding skills in Python and some other coding languages and some python project to practice my coding skills.

Outcome!

If my project is successful, a lot of students are able to get an advantage from EL. They can use EL as a tool to support their studies. By using this application, students will not need to rely on the textbook to study because studying from textbooks will be quite inconvenient sometimes. Furthermore, the app will improve the way of how students learn a lot because it has some tools that can help students manage their studying time. In addition, learning in EF might make students feel interested in studying and this will be the key to help students gain a better learning result.
